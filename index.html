§1Mission ▌ updated 2026·04·27

Flexible artificial intelligence is the ability of an agent to rapidly generalize, adapt, and recombine knowledge to solve novel problems across domains without retraining.

We build the next generation of AI by transforming theories of consciousness into measurable, testable systems — engineering architectures that exhibit compositional, generative representations — and defining flexible intelligence as the core benchmark for real-world adaptability.

// thesis · Consciousness theories are not metaphors. They are architectural specifications. We treat them as engineering inputs and measure what comes out.
